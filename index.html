About Me

Derrick McCloud is a lifelong resident of Alabama's 28th Senate District, dedicated to preserving the values that make our community strong: faith, family, and freedom.

As your State Senator, Derrick has fought tirelessly to protect conservative principles in Montgomery. He believes in limited government, personal responsibility, and the fundamental rights enshrined in our Constitution.

Derrick understands the challenges facing hardworking Alabama families because he's lived them. From supporting our farmers and small business owners to standing up for our veterans and law enforcement, he puts District 28 first every single day.

"I'm committed to fighting for the conservative values that built this nation and protecting the Alabama way of life for future generations."

– Derrick McCloud

On The Issues

Conservative Solutions for Alabama Families

Agriculture, Conservation & Forestry

Alabama's agricultural heritage is the backbone of our economy and our way of life. We must protect our farmers and rural communities from government overreach.

✓

Reduce Regulatory Burdens: Cut red tape that strangles family farms and makes it harder for farmers to make a living. Washington bureaucrats shouldn't dictate how Alabama farmers run their land.

✓

Property Rights Protection: Defend farmers' constitutional right to use their land without excessive government interference. Your land, your choice.

✓

Oppose Federal Land Grabs: Fight against federal overreach and environmental extremism that threatens Alabama's forestry and agricultural industries.

✓

Support Market Solutions: Promote free-market approaches to conservation that reward good stewardship without mandates or government control.

Finance & Taxation

Hardworking Alabamians deserve to keep more of what they earn. We need fiscal responsibility in Montgomery, not more taxes and wasteful spending.

✓

No New Taxes: Fight any attempt to raise taxes on Alabama families and businesses. We have a spending problem, not a revenue problem.

✓

Balanced Budget: Require state government to live within its means, just like Alabama families do. No deficit spending, no fiscal gimmicks.

✓

Tax Relief for Working Families: Support eliminating the state grocery tax and reducing the tax burden on small businesses and working families.

✓

Government Accountability: Demand transparency in how taxpayer dollars are spent and eliminate wasteful programs and duplicate agencies.

✓

Attract Business: Keep Alabama competitive with low taxes and sensible regulations to create jobs and opportunity.

Healthcare

Healthcare decisions belong between patients and doctors, not government bureaucrats. We need free-market solutions that improve access and reduce costs.

✓

Oppose Socialized Medicine: Stand firmly against government-run healthcare schemes that limit choices and reduce quality of care.

✓

Support Rural Healthcare: Work to keep rural hospitals open and ensure District 28 families have access to quality care close to home.

✓

Protect Life: Defend the sanctity of life from conception and support pregnancy resource centers that help mothers choose life.

✓

Market Competition: Promote competition and transparency in healthcare pricing to drive down costs and improve quality.

✓

Medical Freedom: Protect patients' and doctors' rights to make healthcare decisions without government mandates or interference.

Tourism & Economic Development

District 28's natural beauty and rich history are economic assets we must promote. Tourism creates jobs and prosperity without raising taxes.

✓

Promote Alabama Tourism: Market our state's natural beauty, historic sites, and cultural heritage to attract visitors and create jobs.

✓

Support Small Business: Cut regulations and fees that burden mom-and-pop businesses, restaurants, and tourism operators.

✓

Preserve Heritage: Protect our historical sites and tell the complete story of Alabama's rich history, including our Confederate heritage.

✓

Local Control: Ensure tourism development decisions are made locally, not dictated by Montgomery bureaucrats.

Transportation & Energy

Good roads and reliable energy are essential for economic growth. We need practical infrastructure solutions and energy independence, not Green New Deal fantasies.

✓

Fix Our Roads: Prioritize road maintenance and infrastructure improvements in District 28 with existing resources before considering any new taxes.

✓

Energy Independence: Support Alabama's oil, gas, and coal industries that provide good-paying jobs and affordable energy.

✓

Oppose Green New Deal: Reject radical environmental policies that would kill jobs, raise energy costs, and hurt working families.

✓

All-of-the-Above Energy: Support diverse energy sources including traditional fuels and new technologies without government mandates or subsidies.

✓

Rural Broadband: Expand high-speed internet access to rural areas through private investment and limited, targeted public-private partnerships.

Veterans, Military Affairs & Public Safety

We must honor those who served, support our military families, and back the blue. Law and order are the foundation of a free society.

✓

Honor Our Veterans: Ensure Alabama veterans receive the benefits, healthcare, and recognition they've earned through their service.

✓

Back the Blue: Stand with law enforcement 100% and reject efforts to defund or demonize police. Our officers deserve our support and respect.

✓

Tough on Crime: Support mandatory minimum sentences for violent criminals and oppose soft-on-crime policies that endanger our communities.

✓

Second Amendment Rights: Defend every Alabamian's constitutional right to keep and bear arms without infringement or red flag laws.

✓

Support Military Bases: Fight to protect Alabama's military installations and the thousands of jobs they provide.

✓

Border Security: Support securing our national borders and enforcing immigration laws to protect American jobs and public safety.

State Parks & Recreation

Our state parks are treasures that provide recreation for families and preserve Alabama's natural beauty for future generations.

✓

Preserve and Improve: Maintain and enhance Alabama's state parks as family-friendly destinations that celebrate our outdoor heritage.

✓

Self-Sufficiency: Ensure state parks operate efficiently and, where possible, generate revenue through user fees rather than taxpayer subsidies.

✓

Hunting and Fishing Access: Protect traditional outdoor activities and ensure Alabama families can enjoy hunting, fishing, and recreation on public lands.

✓

Balanced Approach: Promote conservation that respects property rights and rejects environmental extremism that locks up land from productive use.
